Rules Reference

Setup

  • Each player starts with 40 HP.
  • Each player starts with 1 hero card and 3 action cards.
  • Both players roll for first turn. Highest roll goes first. Ties re-roll.
  • In PvE, you enter your player name and the game randomly assigns a CPU opponent.

Goal

  • Reduce the enemy player to 0 HP.
  • Heroes at 0 HP go to the graveyard. If a captain dies, another deployed hero can become captain.

Order Phase

  • The Order Phase replaces the old Etiquette Phase name.
  • Players may deploy heroes and buy from the shop.
  • No attacks, hero abilities, role-passive rolls, or action cards are allowed in Order Phase.
  • Roll-Off values provide starting mana for Order Phase setup; Death Die damage begins in Chaos Phase.
  • If Roll-Off has a tie or the final rolls differ by more than 2, each player moves their starting hero into the captain slot for free.
  • Drawing from the Draw Pile costs 0 mana during Order Phase.

Chaos Phase

  • The Chaos Phase replaces the old Combat Phase name.
  • Turns repeat until one player reaches 0 HP.
  • Your turn starts with a Death Die roll after a short countdown. Roll Now skips the wait.
  • Lower HP ranges and major HP deficits improve a player's Card Shop offer tier until they heal back up.

Mana And Shop

  • Mana is spent to deploy heroes, buy from the shop, use abilities, and play action cards.
  • Your mana pool carries over between turns and normally caps at 12.
  • The Card Shop limits purchases by card type each turn. Hero offers deploy immediately for their card cost; action offers cast immediately for their card cost when action cards are legal.
  • Accelerated players or captains gain one extra shop purchase of each type while Accelerated.
  • Some effects can lock the shop or reveal extra offers.
  • Right-click a hand card to discard it for mana, within the current discard limits.

Card Timing

  • Action cards can only be played in the Chaos Phase after rolling the Death Die.
  • Free action cards still follow the same phase restriction.
  • Abstain is free, but it must be played before your major actions for the turn. It draws 2 action cards, strips buffs from your player/captain, prevents player/captain attacks for the turn, and locks further shop/cast actions.
  • Accelerate can target a friendly hero or player unless that target is Charmed. It cancels Impede, Frozen, and Drunk.
  • Augment targets your player/captain or a friendly hero, grants +2 mana, and gives +2 base attack for 3 turns. Player/captain Augment also prevents one failed Death Die damage instance, then expires.
  • Blood Mana grants up to 4 mana, doubled while Crippled or Rabid, then deals scaling backlash. It cannot be cast if the backlash would defeat you.
  • Bombs Away passes a bomb to the enemy's next Death Die roll. Rolling 5-6 defuses it; failing detonates for double the difference and applies Impede.
  • Cheese Potion targets an enemy hero or player, deals 3 damage, and inflicts Edible.
  • Cleanse removes debuffs from a friendly hero or player/captain, then heals based on the number removed.
  • Cripple costs 3 mana, deals 2 initial damage, and inflicts Crippled for 2 turns unless the target is Rabid.
  • Cryofreeze rolls a d6: 4-6 grants +12 Verglas to your player/captain; 1-3 damages and may Freeze only your player/captain.
  • Damage Potion rolls one d6 after hitting up to two targets. On 6, hit targets Burn and your player/captain gain Damage Boost.
  • Drunk Potion targets an enemy hero or player/captain, deals 3 damage, and inflicts Drunk.
  • Gas Potion deals 1 initial damage, Poisons, has a 1/2 chance to spread Poison, and can detonate already Poisoned targets.
  • Impede deals 2 initial damage and disables Sidestep, Evasive Maneuver, and Safeguard without blocking normal attacks or most abilities.
  • Imp-Paired deals 2 initial damage and halves outgoing attack, ability, and action-card damage, rounded down.
  • Love Potion inflicts Charmed. Charmed targets cannot target the charmer or their captain, lose dodge/Safeguard defenses, and successful charms heal the caster 1 HP.
  • Rabies costs 4 mana, deals 4 damage each turn, doubles other incoming damage, can spread each turn, and does not stack separate Poison or Cripple effects.
  • Sidestep grants a guaranteed dodge against the next attack or debuff. Accelerated targets get 2 charges; Safeguard disables Sidestep.
  • Vitalize costs 3 mana, heals 8 HP or 12 HP when critical, cleanses up to 2 non-Rabies debuffs, and grants Vitalized for doubled later healing.

Captains And Roles

  • Drag a deployed hero into the captain slot to activate that hero's role passive.
  • Durability captains can Safeguard, redirecting player or ally damage and debuffs when eligible.
  • Role passives are being revised toward dedicated die events in the 2.4 patch cycle.
  • Damage or debuffs aimed at the player can also affect the captain unless Safeguard changes the target.

Combat

  • Drag a hero onto an enemy hero or HP icon to attack.
  • Click your hero to open its ability panel, then choose a target if the ability is available.
  • Each hero normally performs one action per turn: attack or ability.
  • Accelerate and Speed-role effects can create extra action opportunities. Player or captain Accelerate also increases action-card, hero-cast, player-attack, draw-pile, discard-for-mana, and shop limits by 1.
  • Statuses are shown on cards and player HUDs with turns remaining where applicable.

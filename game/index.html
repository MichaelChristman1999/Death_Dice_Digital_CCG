Rules Reference

Setup

  • Each player starts with 20 HP.
  • Each player draws 3 hero cards and 4 action cards.
  • Both players roll. Highest roll goes first. Ties re-roll.

Core Mechanics

  • Mana is spent to deploy heroes, use hero abilities, buy from the shop, and play paid action cards.
  • Your mana pool carries over between turns and normally caps at 12. Some effects, such as Blood Mana or Mana-role bonuses, can exceed that cap.
  • The captain slot activates that hero's role passive. A Durability captain can Safeguard, taking damage or debuffs for the player or other heroes.
  • Status icons show active buffs and debuffs. Timed statuses count down on the affected player's turns unless a card says otherwise.

Phases

  • Etiquette Phase — One round. Deploy and shop only. No attacks.
  • Combat Phase — Repeat turns until one player reaches 0 HP.

Roll Phase

  • Your turn starts with an automatic dice roll after a short countdown (press Roll Now to skip the wait).
  • Your roll adds that much mana to your pool.
  • If your roll is lower than the previous roll, you take damage equal to the difference.
  • Rolling a 6 resets the requirement for the next player.
  • If the required roll was 5 and you fail it, the Bomb rule can also damage your field.

Your Turn

  • Drag a hero from your hand into your zone to deploy it.
  • Drag a deployed hero into the captain slot to turn on its role passive. Move deployed heroes to rearrange your field.
  • Your player can make a 2 damage base attack by targeting an enemy hero or the enemy HP icon.
  • Each hero may perform one action per turn — attack OR ability.
  • Play one paid action card per turn. Free cards are unlimited.
  • Accelerate and some Speed-role effects can grant extra action-card or action opportunities.
  • Locked Out disables shop access while it lasts.
  • Buy one card from the shop per turn (hero: 2◆, action: 1◆).
  • Right-click a hand card to discard it for its mana value (1 hero + 1 action per turn).

Status Basics

  • Accelerate grants extra action potential and cancels Impede. Impede blocks attacks and abilities and cancels Accelerate.
  • Cripple makes incoming damage hurt more. Anemic blocks healing. Impaired lowers damage output.
  • Poison, Burning, and Haunted deal damage over time. Burning and Frozen cancel each other.
  • Sidestep gives a dodge roll against the next incoming attack or debuff.
  • Blessed and Cursed are opposite swing statuses and cancel each other when applied.

Card Reading

  • Hero cards show cost, attack, HP, role icon, and ability or passive text.
  • Click a hand card or deployed hero to read its details, including role passive information.
  • If a rule on a card conflicts with this reference, use the card text for that playtest.

Combat

  • Drag your character onto an enemy card — or their HP icon — to attack.
  • Click your character to open its ability panel, then pick a target.
  • Click an action card to play it — the game tells you what to target.
  • Statuses are shown on cards with turns remaining (☠ poison ticks every turn!).
  • Attacking or using an ability taps the character (turns sideways) until your next turn.
  • Damage and debuffs aimed at the player can also affect the captain. Safeguard redirects them to a Durability captain unless that captain is disabled.
  • 0 HP → graveyard. Reduce the enemy player to 0 HP to win!

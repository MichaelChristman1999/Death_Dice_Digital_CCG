# Death Dice — Character Ability Design Outline (from the designer)

> Source of truth for character abilities, supplied 2026-06-13. Implementation
> lives in `js/heroAbilities.js`; effect handlers in `js/abilityDispatcher.js`.

- Shish-Ke-Bob: once per turn, destroy edible character
- Gorgon-Zola: Applies edible status
- Hip-Hop-Papa: Duel challenger
- In-Specter: 5 card reveal
- Riff Wrath: Damage boost L/R (adjacent allies)
- Ruby Goldberg: Cascade damage
- Lone Wolf: Bombs away amplified
- Marionetta: Control enemy character
- Determinator: Sacrifice to apply survive
- Giant Jess: Throw Enemy Characters
- Kevlard: No tap to block
- Lassquatch: Defender donates action card or takes +Damage
- Juju Jitsu: Swap status effects
- Zoomstick: Ally attacks twice
- Wind Breaker: Randomized gas effect (1-6)
- Mr. Immutable: immune to status effects
- Chill Seeker (Bro Chill): Remove status effects from all
- Iron Maid: Remove negative status effects
- Bearzerk: Sacrifice damage to player
- Val-Cano: Burn defender action cards
- Geezer Freezer: Incoming damage freeze (50% vs Duel)
- Cutlass: Action card wager duel
- Copy Cat: Steal action cards played, character taps
- Breast Knuckle: Duel for double damage
- Tyrannosaurus: Team boost, enrage
- Fryborg: Grease based aoe effects
- Wei-fu: Dodge counterattack chance
- Baller-ina: 33% evade, blaze opponents dodged
- Beeatrice: Apply nectar to allies or enemies
- Aegalien: Laser shots and migraine dealer
- Ghoulia: Ex-hex parasitic effect
- Stall-Wart: Doesn't tap to defend / +Atk for block
- Equinox: Accelerate/Damage Boost Team
- Yeast Priest: Blessing ability
- Troll-Net: Causes 2 other characters to duel
- Disc Jockey: innate acceleration / accel or heal allies
- Goosebump: 5/6 Apply Rabies, neg status effects buff his attack
- Slendeer: multi-hit, potential combos
- Astroid (Aster Roid): 2x dmg / Self Heal
- Cheata (Cheatah): take 3 cards from an opponent, keep one
- Chiki Barstooli: Barstool Blitz tackle (tap) target
- Cath-eine: Agility dealer to allies, self heal, self damage if no coffee/turn
- Prowl-Ball: unblockable projectile (bypass guard)
- Afrodisiac: Love status dealer
- Mumma-mia: AOE damage w/ baby / single target post baby
- Mob Barley: Drunk status dealer / drinking game mechanic
- Dreadlocks: place on top of draw deck to lockdown
- Trophy Wife: Gains enchantment from duel
- Shell Shocked: Chain lightning multiple attack
- Sir Ringe: Multi status dealing
